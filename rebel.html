Everybody plays it safe. Your clients are exhausted by safe. The industry is full of brands that look like each other, sound like each other, and charge like each other. You walk in and say "that's not the only way." That's not a niche - that's the whole opportunity.
What your website actually looks and feels like
Corners
Razor sharp everywhere. Rounded corners are for friendly brands. You are not a friendly brand - you are a point of view. Every sharp edge says we made a decision and we're not apologizing for it.
Animation - hover this
Things snap and appear - no gentle fades. The page feels like it has an attitude. This card switches aggressively on hover. No easing in. No apology.
Dividers
Thick borders used as confrontation - like WARNING label borders. They divide things loudly. Something is about to change.
