The thing that makes a Caregiver brand different
Most brands talk about what they do. You talk about how people feel. That's not a soft version of business - it's the whole point. The clients who need you most are looking for safety before they can even think about results. You give them that without trying.
What your website actually looks and feels like
Corners
Softly rounded - like the corner of a well-used greeting card. 8-12px border radius. Approachable, safe, held. Sharp corners would feel too clinical for someone who makes people feel cared for.
Animation
Very gentle fades and drifts - like a door opening slowly. Nothing bounces or pops. Everything eases in as if it has time for you. The pace of the page should feel unhurried.
Photos
Warm, personal, human. Genuine moments over staged shots. Photos where you can feel the care in them. If it feels like a stock photo of someone looking helpful, don't use it.
