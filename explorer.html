The thing that makes a Explorer brand different
Most brands tell people where to go. You take them somewhere they couldn't have gotten to alone. There's a difference between a GPS and a guide. Your clients aren't just following directions - they're trusting you with the territory. That relationship is rarer and worth more than any deliverable.
What your website actually looks and feels like
Corners
Sharp everywhere. This brand is going somewhere and doesn't have time to be soft. Sharp edges signal direction and intention - like a compass needle.
Animation - hover this
Things arrive from the side - forward momentum, not upward or outward. This card slides sideways on hover. Always directional. The page feels like motion.
Photos
Landscapes, journeys, movement. Wide open spaces. The photos should make you want to go somewhere. Never static, never posed in a studio.
