The thing that makes a Innocent brand different
In a world full of brands trying to be impressive, you're trying to be clear. That sounds like settling but it's actually harder - and rarer. Clarity requires confidence. You have to know exactly what you do and say it without hedging. That kind of directness is disarming. People trust it immediately.
What your website actually looks and feels like
Corners
Nicely rounded - like the corners of a greeting card. 12-16px radius. Not sharp like a legal document, not perfectly circular. Soft enough to feel approachable and safe.
Animation - hover this
Gentle floating or soft fade-ins. Nothing startling or sudden. The page should feel like a warm hug. This card rises gently - that's the entire animation vocabulary for an Innocent brand.
Photos
Friendly illustrations or real candid moments. A little imperfect, a lot real. The photo that looks like it was taken by a friend beats the photo that cost $2,000.
