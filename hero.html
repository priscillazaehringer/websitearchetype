Voice and tone
Direct, confident, results-focused. Short, punchy sentences. Active voice always. Your copy should feel like a coach who believes in you but won't let you make excuses.
DO THIS
"You've done the hard part. Now let's build the brand that proves it."
NOT THIS
"We'd love to help you on your journey towards maybe possibly reaching your goals someday!"
