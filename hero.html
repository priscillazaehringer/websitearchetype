Act 03
Your brand in one paragraph.
Paste this at the start of any AI prompt. ChatGPT, Claude, whatever you use. Every caption, email, and sales page you write from here will sound like you.
My brand archetype is The Hero (The War Room). My brand voice is direct, confident, and results-focused. I use short, punchy sentences. Active voice always. I assume capability in my audience: no hand-holding, no hedging, no filler. My visual identity uses dark backgrounds, amber accents, and cream tones. Sharp corners everywhere, tight purposeful whitespace, and animations that move upward. My typography pairs a heavy serif for headlines with a clean sans-serif for body. My brand energy feels like a starting gun: earned, not given. When writing for my brand, channel the energy of someone who has done the work and is here to help others do the same. No excuses, no fluff, just results.
