The thing that makes a Sage brand different
Most brands in your space promise outcomes. You give people understanding. That's not a softer version of the same thing - it's a fundamentally different relationship. When clients understand why something works, they trust you more, refer you more, and stay longer.
What your website actually looks and feels like
Corners
Sharp everywhere. This is a brand that values precision - soft rounded corners would feel too casual, like a professor showing up in flip-flops. Sharp corners signal deliberate decisions.
White space
Maximum breathing room - like a textbook with wide margins so you can think while you read. Generous space says what's here is worth focusing on.
Animation
Very subtle - text fades in gently as you scroll. Nothing bouncy or dramatic. Movement should feel like turning a page, not a performance. Restraint is intentional.
